Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how UniPlay LTD ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the UniPlay desktop utility. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Information We Collect

To provide high-performance optimization and analytics, we collect the following data:

  • Account Information: Name and email address provided during the purchase process via our Merchant of Record.
  • Unique Hardware Identifier (UUID): A non-reversible hash of your hardware components used to verify license authenticity and prevent unauthorized distribution.
  • Steam ID (Public): Used to synchronize game library metadata and provide per-game performance profiles.
  • Technical Analytics: Data regarding RAM reclamation, process priority shifts, and session duration to populate your "Ledger" dashboard.

2. Lawful Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual Necessity: To provide the Pro features (Analytics, Ledger, Profiles) you have subscribed to.
  • Legitimate Interests: To protect our intellectual property (the Decoupled Bridge Architecture) and ensure software security via hardware-locked licensing.

3. Data Retention & Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We only share information with essential service providers:

  • Lemon Squeezy, LLC: Our Merchant of Record handles all payment processing. UniPlay LTD does not store or see your credit card or billing details.
  • Security: Hardware IDs are stored as long as an account is active to prevent license fraud.

4. Your Rights

As a UK resident, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to rectification if your data is inaccurate.
  • The right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") of your account and associated analytics.
  • The right to restrict or object to certain processing activities.

5. Data Security

Your analytics data is processed locally by the UniPlay Scout Thread (Rust) before being synchronized with your account. We implement industry-standard encryption to protect data in transit between the client and our servers.

6. Contact & Controller

UniPlay LTD is the data controller for your information.

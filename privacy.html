2. Information We Collect, Use, and Why

Services and Goods

We collect or use the following information to provide services, including software licensing:

  • Names and contact details
  • Purchase or account history
  • Website user information (user journeys and cookie tracking)

Crime Prevention & Security

We collect or use the following information to prevent, detect, or prosecute crimes (including IP theft and fraud):

  • Names and contact information
  • Financial transaction information
  • Publicly available Steam ID
  • Unique Hardware ID

Legal Requirements & Claims

We collect information to comply with legal obligations (tax/HMRC) and for dealing with queries or claims, including correspondence and financial records.

3. Lawful Bases and Data Protection Rights

Under UK GDPR, we rely on the following lawful bases:

  • Consent: Where you have given clear permission.
  • Contract: Necessary to fulfill our agreement with you.
  • Legal Obligation: Necessary to comply with the law.
  • Legitimate Interests: Specifically protecting our intellectual property and providing customer support.

Your Data Rights

You have the right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, objection, and data portability. If we rely on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

4. Where We Get Information From

Directly from you, and from third parties including Cookies/Analytics and our Merchant of Record, Lemon Squeezy, who provides transaction details following a purchase.

5. Retention and Sharing

We keep license data for the duration of your active license, financial records for 6 years (HMRC), and support emails for 2 years.

We share data with Lemon Squeezy, LLC in the United States. This transfer complies with UK law via the UK-US Data Bridge (Adequacy Regulations).
